About Us

About our company

Pronto was born 2019, out of passion,tenacity,teamworkand the will-power to filla vital gap in the value chain of ecommerce, technology and logistics industry of the most opulous black nation on earth.
Pronto interest remains in the five different fields of logistics while presently concentrating on sales,procurement and recovery logistics.

We want to change the everyday narrative hence "Pronto" Italian word meaning quickly without delay or on time. We deliver happiness to every door step in the safest and best possible time to help businesses complete their value chain with the highest satisfaction

To achieve this we must work together. "If we cannot be quicker than we must be at the right place at the right time" time is valuable and key to logistics. Logistics is a key factor to trade and commerce. Trade is what unites the world against all our differences.

We believe that our continued success will come from th strength of team work. Innovation technology and never undermining the faintest of positive contributions and criticism. We are engineered to solve problems. To know more about us contact us.
