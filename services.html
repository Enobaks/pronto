Our Services

Our great services

As a leading logistic service provider in Nigeria, we are known for our ability to handle every last detail of our customers needs.
Pronto logistics takes care of all your logistics.
We have dedicated employees working tirelessly to make sure we deliver operational excellence.



PRONTO-DISPATCH: This is an on-demand pick up and delivery service available to the inhabitants of Warri and environs. This service is available to individuals, start-ups or SMEs; whether you are a food vendor, baker, fashion designers, boutique owner etc, we have motorcycles and vehicles to help you safely and conveniently deliver to your customers no matter the size and volume.

PRONTO-NIGERIA: This is a nation-wide door step delivery service. We look for the most affordable and convenient way for delivering your packages around Nigeria. This service is charged using weight and it takes 2-4 working days.

PRONTO-INTERNATIONAL: We help our client send and receive parcels in and out Nigeria. Delivery time frame is between 5-7 working days.

PRONTO-HAULAGE : Relocating to a new city or you’re looking for a way to move your goods in bulk around the country? Our PRONTO-haulage is for you, call to hire a truck or van.

PRONTO-CARGO: We don’t believe borders should restrict your business. This service is for clients looking to export and import goods in large quantities. Our freight services are superb. We handle the documentation, shipping and clearing.
